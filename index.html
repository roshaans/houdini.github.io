Welcome to Houdini Music

A music app that learns your taste and reccomends only your favorite songs.

AI algorithm

Upon starting the app, you will be greeted with a set of songs. This, along with your music listening activity will allow Houdini to reccomend you soul touching songs.

Control Sound Quality

Song has too little bass? No worries because with Houdini, you can add boost your bass, control sorround sound, and even choose from the avalaible reverb presets!

Listen to songs offline

No need to use your crucial data on your music anymore. Easily download hundreds of songs onto the app and take them with you wherever you go.

Social Media Platform

Music can be a social activity and we realize that at Houdini. This is why we have built a series of tools and features for you to interact with other users and curate playlists together.

Give the app a try on your platform of choice! We also have a desktop application!
